Final Thoughts

While showing it's age, Final Fantasy: The Spirits Within is still a technological marvel of it's time, and most of the CGI still holds up these days. However, it's not a particularly good film; the story is a bit nonsensical, and the incredible talent that was brought on for the voice acting all sound a bit bored or forced - there is no chemistry between them. It is a fun watch for fans of the series, and, on that, it's incredibly cheap to find these days, so collectors shouldn't be remiss about picking up a copy.
