Final Thoughts

Everything about Battle Arena Toshinden is incredibly dull. The story is difficult to parse for a fan of the series like myself, and nonsensical to everyone else. The writing is awful, and the voice acting is laughable at best. The animation could have been the saving grace, but even it is subpar. Only the most devout fans of the series should consider watching it, and, even then, should prepare themselves to be disappointed.
