About us

Abysmal Animation Adaptations is a small theater and video store in Somewhere, Someplace, dedicated to bringing you the best of the worst in animated movies and television shows, particularly spinoffs from other series. We specialize in finding the Least Of The East, the Unblessed From The West, and anything in between! Come laugh, have fun, and engage in thoughtful discussion on unnecessary corporatization of subcultures! Looking for an obscure title from overseas? Check out our library of purchasable home movies. See our Reviews page for humourous musings on horrendous derivatives, or our monthly calendar to see what events and movie showings are planned for this month, or our hours of operation.
